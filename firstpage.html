ANNOUNCEMENTS

Troop 1142 Takes Simple Meals Badge

Our girls are currently working on earning the Simple Meals Badge which will allow them to acquire basic cooking skills. The badge consists of 5 requirements, and the girls have already finished requirement #1 which was a visit to the Chick-fil-A kitchen. Requirements 2-4 will completed during the overnight, and requirement #5 which involves making your own meal, will be completed individually at home.



2017 Fall Product Season

This year's Fall Product Season will be launching on October 1, with the overall goal of enhancing the girls' leadership skills. 5 skills the girls will work on include goal setting, decision making, money management, people skills, and business ethics. Additionally, the Fall Product Program will give girls the opportunity to sell magazine subscriptions, nuts, and candies in order to earn some start-up money for the troop.
